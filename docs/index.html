The pilot's headline finding is methodological: comparing the six-group score spread (MASD) to a two-run pairwise noise floor overstates disparity ~2.4× through statistic arity alone. Against an arity-matched noise floor and an omnibus group test, claude-haiku-4-5 shows no demographic effect above sampling noise (arity-matched MASD/noise ratio mean 0.83, below 1 in every cell; 0 of 120 pairwise and 0 of 9 omnibus contrasts survive FDR).
